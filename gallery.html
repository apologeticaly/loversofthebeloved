The name “Muhammad” literally translates into English as “The Praised One.” Allah Almighty in His Holy Qur’an says, “Indeed, Allah confers blessing upon the Prophet, and His angels [ask Him to do so]. O you who have believed, ask [Allah to confer] blessing upon him and ask [Allah to grant him] peace” (Qur’an 33:56). This commandment from our Lord was the motivation for creating the group Ahbab Al-Habib.
