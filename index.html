About Us

Ahbab Al-Habib (Lovers of the Beloved) is a nasheed group based in California that is dedicated to spreading the love of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ through the art and tradition of classical Inshād (spiritual odes). 

Recognizing that we live in an age when intimate knowledge of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and a celebration of his life is sorely lacking, Ahbab Al-Habib strives to fill this void by teaching society about the most perfect human being to ever have lived — our beloved Prophet Muhammad ﷺ — by joyfully singing his praises in gatherings that are arranged in his honor.
