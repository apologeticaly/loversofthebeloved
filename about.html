About Us

Early on, our members were blessed to recognize the value of truly knowing who Rasool Allah ﷺ is and learning about the gifts and guidance that he offered the world. They decided to travel overseas to attain the love of the Messenger ﷺ, finding it to be prevalent in the art of traditional Inshād (spiritual odes). They dedicated themselves to years of intensive study and training with some of the greatest Munshideen (masters of spiritual odes) of the modern era from Syria and Jordan. Their teachers include Shaykh Samer Nashar, Shaykh Muhammad Adel, Shaykh Jihad Al-Kalouti, Shaykh Abdullah Adel, Shaykh Abdulrahman Sha’ar, Shaykh Salah Al-Kurdy, and Ustadh Omar Helal.

Abdul-Majeed Abugideiri

Abdul-Majeed Abugideiri has been studying the art of traditional inshad and singing intensively since he was five years old in Amman, Jordan. His parents and Qur’an teachers realized early on that he had a special talent for music and singing. He has studied for the past 15 years with some of the most prominent munshideen (nasheed singers) from Shaam (Levant Region); under their tutelage, he sang in various zawiyas and performed at various events throughout Jordan and internationally. Some of the countries Abdul Majeed has performed in include, Egypt, Turkey, Bosnia, Saudi Arabia, Pakistan, Canada, Jordan, the United States, and the United Kingdom. Abdul-Majeed now resides in California and hopes to spread his love for traditional spiritual poetry and the praises of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ throughout the world.

Ibrahim Khan

Ibrahim Khan was born and raised in Southern California. At the age of 13, he moved to Amman, Jordan, to study the traditional Islamic sciences. He quickly discovered a passion for traditional inshad and started studying with various munshideen (nasheed singers). Along with singing, Ibrahim studied hand-drumming intensively. For over five years in Jordan, Ibrahim has been drumming at mawlids, weddings, and other religious events. He is the lead drummer for Ahbab Al-Habib. You can always find him with a duff (hand drum) in hand. Ibrahim currently resides in California where he sings with Ahbab Al-Habib and performs at mawlids and events to help spread the love of the Prophet ﷺ.

Shaan Mukhtar

Shaan Mukhtar grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area where at a very young age he was exposed to mawlids and traditional Islamic poetry. With the encouragement of his teachers, Shaan took a gap year during college to go to Jordan to study Arabic and inshad. There, he met Abdul-Majeed and Ibrahim and started learning classical singing from them and their teachers. For one year, Shaan studied in Jordan extensively. He has now returned to California where he sings at mawlids and events throughout the Bay Area.
